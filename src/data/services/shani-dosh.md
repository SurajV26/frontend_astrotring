## What Is Shani (Saturn) in Vedic Astrology?

In **Jyotish Shastra**, Shani (Saturn) is simultaneously described as one of the most powerful, most dangerous, and most straightforward planets in the horoscope.

It governs:

- **Makar Rashi (Capricorn)** and **Kumbh Rashi (Aquarius)**
- Karma, discipline, hard work, delays, justice, and time
- Wealth, real estate, oil, iron, coal, labor, and agriculture
- The natural significator of bones, teeth, joints, longevity, and the nervous system

Saturn does not believe in luck, shortcuts, or freebies.

It is the planet of **earned outcomes** — what you put in is exactly what you get out.

This unflinching quality is why Saturn is the most feared planet in astrology — and also the most rewarding one for those willing to do the work.

## What Is Shani Dosh? Definition and Meaning

**Shani Dosh** refers to the negative or afflicted influence of Saturn in a person's birth chart (Kundli).

It is considered present when:

- Saturn is debilitated in Aries (Mesh Rashi)
- Saturn occupies challenging houses — particularly the 3rd, 6th, 8th, or 12th house
- Saturn is conjunct or aspected by malefic planets like Rahu, Ketu, or Mars
- Shani Sade Sati or Shani Dhaiya is active over the natal Moon
- Saturn is the lord of difficult houses placed unfavorably in the chart

When Shani Dosh is present, life feels heavy:

- Constant delays
- Unexpected obstacles
- Working twice as hard for half the results

This is Saturn testing you.

## Shani Sade Sati: The 7.5-Year Saturn Transit

Shani Sade Sati occurs when Saturn transits:

1. The sign before your Moon
2. The sign of your Moon
3. The sign after your Moon

Total duration: **7.5 years**

### Three Phases:

**Phase 1 – Rising Phase**  
Mental pressure, career uncertainty.

**Phase 2 – Peak Phase**  
Deep emotional transformation.

**Phase 3 – Setting Phase**  
Gradual relief and rebuilding.

During Sade Sati:

- People change jobs
- Change professions
- Change cities
- Change identities

Saturn pushes transformation.
