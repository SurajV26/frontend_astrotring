# What Is Grahan Dosh? Definition and Meaning in Vedic Astrology

The word **Grahan (ग्रहण)** means _eclipse_.

Just as the Sun and Moon experience eclipses in the sky when Rahu or Ketu shadow them, the same effect can occur in a birth chart when the Sun or Moon is conjunct with or aspected by Rahu or Ketu.

### Astronomical Terms:

- A **Lunar Eclipse** occurs when Earth's shadow falls on the Moon
- A **Solar Eclipse** occurs when the Moon blocks the Sun

### Vedic Astrology Terms:

- **Chandra Grahan Dosh (Lunar Eclipse Dosh)** → Moon afflicted by Rahu or Ketu
- **Surya Grahan Dosh (Solar Eclipse Dosh)** → Sun afflicted by Rahu or Ketu

Rahu and Ketu are shadow planets (Chhaya Grahas). When they engulf the Sun or Moon, they block natural light and energy — causing deep disruption in life.
